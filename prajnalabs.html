GG-MAP Portal.

The Goaf-Gas Migration Analysis Portal (GG-MAP). A high-fidelity industrial asset delivering species transport modeling for longwall mining operations, bridging the gap between subsurface physics and site-ready decision support.

GG-MAP: Migration Resolving

GG-MAP resolves the migration pathways of methane and oxygen within the collapsed goaf. By integrating site-specific dimensions with DNS-validated steady-state models, the portal provides a high-scent visual of accumulation zones and inertisation effectiveness.

Industrial Data Deployment

Designed for site-level ventilation officers, GG-MAP simplifies complex species transport into interactive contour plots. It eliminates the need for theoretical guesswork, providing a verifiable empirical record for mine safety planning.

Physics Engine Trajectory

Steady-State Logic (Current)

GG-MAP currently utilizes industry-accepted steady-state physics models to provide rapid, scenario-based insights. This ensures engineering integrity through high-fidelity visualizations of gas migration.

PINN Predictive Analysis (Future)

Planned integration of Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) will enable real-time, predictive gas distribution modeling, accelerating high-fidelity interpolation for dynamic mining environments.
