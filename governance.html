Innovation
Working Model.

Our model ensures that scientific insight transitions into societal evidence through three distinct operational pathways, each structured for mutual benefit and statutory finality.

Property Rights ★

Formal assignment of technical assets to the Studio. Per Section 6 of the Patents Act, this establishes a clear chain-of-title for future exits.

Economic Settlement §

Rewards are treated as Full & Final Settlement via pre-defined formulas. Moral Equity remains the primary legacy in non-commercial outcomes.

Moral Equity † ‡

Verification of True and First Inventor status. Alignment with IEEE & CRediT ensures non-negotiable professional recognition.

Grant Compliance

Adherence to ANRF / Central Govt rules. Overheads follow agency-mandated caps to ensure scientific accountability.

Collaborative IP ★

Tripartite IP management with host institutions. Studio acts as Industrial Partner for exclusive commercial scaling.

Grant PI Roles †

Senior technologists and partners lead as formal PI/Co-PI, meeting the highest standards of scientific reporting.

Academic Gain

Institutions gain industrial-linked research outputs, verified student placement data, and NIRF-relevant industrial collaborations.

MOU Statutory Basis

Projects are governed by a legally binding MOU (Contract Act, 1872). IP is managed via joint-ownership or exclusive industrial licensing, ensuring commercial clarity from day one.
